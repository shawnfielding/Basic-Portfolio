About Me

Shawn Fielding has lived many professional lives already:

  • Professional Librarian
  • Program and Project Manager
  • Department Manager
  • Knowledge and Enterprise Content Manager

He tends to overdo things until they are better than requested, which has pretty much meant that he worked himself out of a job a couple times now in the content management area.

So Shawn, even with the babyface, is going through a midlife crisis and realising this is his last chance to do what he loves. Shawn realized that he is happiest when he is playing with code directly.

However, his skills are from a time that even apps really were not a thing. So he is refreshing, getting back to mastery.

So he is making the blind leap. He sure hopes that it works out.
