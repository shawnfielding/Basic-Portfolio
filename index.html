About Me

Shawn Fielding has lived many professional lives already:

  • Professional Librarian
  • Program and Project Manager
  • Department Manager
  • Knowledge and Enterprise Content Manager

However, due to being too good at the last one, he has worked himself out of job twice.

Therefore it was time for a midlife crisis. He realized that if didn't do the thing that brings him joy as a profession now, it would never happen.

So he is making the blind leap. He sure hopes that it works out.
